About

  • Creative Development

    This section focuses on crafting the anime's foundation. Writers develop the script, storyboard artists plan scenes, character designers create unique personas, and concept artists establish the visual style.

  • Animation

    Animation directors supervise the animators, ensuring consistent and high-quality character movements. Key animators create essential frames, inbetween animators fill in details, and background artists design scenes for depth.

  • Production

    Production coordinators manage schedules and budgets, while assistants aid in communication and paperwork. Quality control experts ensure the final product meets the company's standards.

  • Post-Production

    Editors assemble final scenes, add effects, and synchronize audio. Sound designers work on music, sound effects, and voiceovers. Localization experts adapt content for international audiences.

  • Marketing and Distribution

    Marketing teams create promotional materials, and distribution managers plan release strategies, negotiating deals with platforms for global accessibility.

  • Management and Administration

    Human resources handle staff matters, legal teams manage contracts and rights, and finance teams ensure financial stability.
